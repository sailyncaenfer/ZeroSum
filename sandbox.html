Expectimax AI

The AI runs a depth-limited expectimax search: it maximizes its own move, averages over sampled random spawns (weighted toward small combinable values and a representative large value), and scores boards by empty space, total merge potential, and total remaining magnitude. Use AI Step to make one AI move at a time without auto-play.

How This Works

Swipe or use arrow keys / WASD to slide every tile in one direction. Tiles glide until they hit the edge of the board or another tile.

Cancel: two tiles that are exact opposites (equal magnitude, opposite sign) vanish when pushed together.

Combine: a tile at or under the combine threshold can fuse into any neighbor it's pushed into (as long as the resulting sum doesn't exceed the current max tile value), becoming one tile holding their sum.

Everything here — board size, tile ranges, scaling, merge rules, spawn behavior, and the AI — is adjustable from the gear (⚙) menu. Nothing is fixed.
